**Tips for Recording:**
1. **Pacing:** Speak clearly and leave pauses for the AI agent's responses.
2. **Lighting:** Ensure your face is well-lit so the "Vision" part of the demo is clear.
3. **Audio:** Use a good microphone; the Gemini Live API sounds best when your input is crisp!
